Brown DePrez & Johnson P.A.

Brown, DePrez & Johnson, P.A. is a professional association of attorneys that has been serving Central Indiana for more than 160 years. Our extensive practice areas, combined with our personal touch, make us a good fit for both individuals and businesses in need of legal advice. We are conveniently located in two buildings within a block of the Shelby County Court House.

We offer our services in central Indiana including the following counties and their cities:

  • Shelby County (Shelbyville)
  • Decatur County (Greensburg)
  • Hancock County (Greenfield)
  • Johnson County (Franklin and Greenwood)
  • Rush County (Rushville)
  • Fayette County (Connersville)
  • Jennings County (Vernon)
  • Ripley County (Versailles and Batesville)
  • Bartholomew County (Columbus)
  • Jackson County (Brownstown and Seymour)

If you need legal assistance, help is as close as a visit to our office.
