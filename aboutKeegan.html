Riley Keegan Sullivan

Phone (317) 398-6687
Fax (317) 398-6806

As the newest addition to the firm, Riley ‘Keegan’ Sullivan is a general practicing attorney with a concentration in such areas as family law, criminal law, civil law, and wills, trusts, and estates. Keegan is a Noblesville, Indiana native and received his undergraduate education from Wabash College in Crawfordsville, Indiana, graduating in 2013 with a major in Religion and a minor in History. Through his religious studies, Keegan was presented with the opportunity to embark on a mission trip to the country of Kenya in East Africa. While abroad, he visited various Catholic Churches, schools, and orphanages, while also getting the chance to meet with different religious figures. His time in Kenya also included interacting with natives and assisting with manual labor. Keegan attributes this experience as a major spark in his long-established passion for helping those in need that might not be in a position to help themselves. Keegan then went on to study law in Dayton, Ohio at the University of Dayton School of Law, graduating in 2020.

While studying law, Keegan achieved valuable experience as an extern for the Montgomery County Prosecutor’s Office in Ohio. He was also given the opportunity to gain experience in his own law interests while interning/externing at an Ohio firm that focuses on family law, civil law, and wills, trusts, and estates. Keegan has recently returned to his home state of Indiana to begin his career as an attorney for Brown, DePrez, & Johnson P.A.
